ABOUT

HAPR (Health At Priority) is the one-stop solution for disease prediction. HAPR is equipped with the best dataset and is very reliable. Let's start the journey. Our motto is Health First. Whenever in doubt about your health, HAPR should be the first tap.It briefs you about the illness that you might be suffering from or what are the chances of the diseases you might possess unknowingly. HAPR shows you the possibilities by considering the symptoms that you fill in at first. HAPR will also let you know whether you are undergoing depression or experiencing anxiety, and how severe it is.
To improve HAPR we need your support. Let us know how you felt after using HAPR and what refinement it needs.
